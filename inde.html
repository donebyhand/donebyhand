Hey There Future Jellyfish Expert...

Are you fascinated by the "ghosts of the sea?" Jellyfish are known to be one of the most intriguing marine creatures, especially since they have been inhabiting our oceans for over 500 million years. Despite lacking brains, hearts, and bones, these gelatinous beings still respond to their environment via a single nervous system. Although many are intimidated by the enticing stingers on these creatures, jellyfish play a very important role within the marine ecosystems.

Explore our site to take a deeper dive into everything jellyfish-related! We strive to teach the world about jellyfish, no matter if you are a student, a marine expert, or a novice. However, there are many common misconceptions behind these gentle creatures, so please take the time to learn firsthand through Jellyfish Journal.
